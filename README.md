# E-Commerce-Sales-Profitability-Simulated-Retail-PlatformTools: Power BI, SQL, Excel | Dataset: 50,000 transactions, 9,924 customers
• Gathered and documented business requirements from simulated product, marketing, and finance stakeholders; translated requirements into PRD and user stories for dashboard development.
• Designed Figma wireframes and user flows for the executive dashboard (8+ KPIs: AOV Rs.50.25K, Revenue/Customer Rs.253K) before development — enabling faster sign-off and reduced rework.
• Modelled data using BPMN to map the end-to-end order-to-revenue process and identified profit leakage at the discount tier (20%+ discount bucket driving Rs.1.07B revenue while compressing margins).
• Analysed Rs.2.51B revenue across 4 regions and 5 product categories; delivered 4 strategic recommendations on discount optimisation, regional marketing focus, customer retention, and category reallocation.
• Segmented customers by order frequency, identifying 'power customers' (10+ orders) and recommending a VIP loyalty programme to improve retention and revenue per customer.
